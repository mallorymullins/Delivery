Chapter 4: The Garden

You decide to make a break for the side door. You have never run as fast as you have now. You make it to the door and feel you legs move even faster. You know this isnt over till your back in your car. You suddenly hear a thud behind you. You know you shouldn't look back but you do...

When you glance over your shoulder you see the woman's hand pressed against the window.

Even though it's just an hand, you know it has a deeper meaning. You think to youself, Thank god I went for the door.

You jump higher than you ever had and climb over the gate. You feel a sense of relief as you see your car.
